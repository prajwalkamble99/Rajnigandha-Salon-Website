About Us

INTRODUCING OUR ICONIC RAJNIGANDHA SALON

Rajnigandha Salon and Academy is a full service women’s hair salon located in Wardha, Maharashtra, India.

Our professional team is current with all the latest styles and trends to provide you with your best look. We specialize in Deva cuts, single-process color, highlighting, and Pintura. Stop in to schedule a consultation or book online today!

We strive for the highest quality of service, while maintaining a fun, friendly, non-pretentious environment.

Meet Our Founder

Mrs. Bharti Kamble

Founder- Rajnigandha Salon & Academy

Bharti Kamble is founder of Rajnigandha salon and academy, who has 40 years of experience in salons. Although much of her role entails overseeing the salon, she has a passion for hair styling, and takes into account the demands of modern women’s lifestyles, along with the need for low-maintenance, easy management and versatility. She has a particular passion for giving more mature women a modern style that can reflect their true confidence, allowing them to look chic, yet youthful.
